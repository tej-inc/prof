Tj is a graphics and arts designer, specializing in the delicate construction of immaculate and purposeful websites\web applications for clients of all forms and sizes.Tj is capable within a range of tactical and big picture designs, in other words, i'm a seasoned professional!.

ROOTS

tj is passionate about making sure that systems, interfaces, language and graphics are human-friendly, emotive, aesthetically pleasing, clear, on-brand and usable with a touch of mellow smoothness. He has worked in a variety of environments, from mega-consultancies to small design shops to product companies.
